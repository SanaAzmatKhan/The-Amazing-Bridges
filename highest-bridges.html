The Aizhai Bridge
is a suspension bridge on the G65 Baotou–Maoming Expressway near Jishou, Hunan, China.
The bridge was built as part of an expressway from southwest China's Chongqing Municipality to Changsha.
Length: 1534.00 m.
Width: 24.5.
Opening Date: 2012-03-31.

The Millau Viaduct
is a cable-stayed road-bridge that spans the valley of the river Tarn near Millau in southern France.
Construction began in October of 2001 and took a little over 3 years.
It is also the 12th highest bridge in the world.
Height: 270 m.
Total length: 2460 m.
The bridge was opened in 2004.


The Royal Gorge Bridge
is a tourist attraction near Cañon City,
Colorado within Royal Gorge Bridge & Park,
a 360-acre amusement park located along the edge of the Royal Gorge around both ends of the bridge.
Location: 4218 County Road 3a, Canon City, CO 81212-3663.

The Nanpu Bridge,
in Shanghai, China, sister bridge to the Yangpu Bridge, is one of the main bridges in Shanghai.
Total length: 8,346 m.
Construction started: December 15, 1988.
Opened: December 1, 1991.
Height: 150 m.
Bridge type: Cable-stayed bridge.
