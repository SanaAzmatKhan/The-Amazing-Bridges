Q: What is bridges?

A: A bridge is a structure built to span physical obstacles without closing the way underneath such as a body of water, valley, or road, for the purpose of providing passage over the obstacle.

Q:How many types of bridges?

A: Bridges can be categorized in several different ways.
1: Structure type. 2: Double-decked bridges.
3: Fixed or movable bridges. 4: Three-way bridges.
5: Viaducts.

Q:Who built the first bridge?

A:The First Bridge was Built in Telford in 1779 by Abraham Darby.

Q:Where is the Golden Gate Bridge located?

A:The golden gate bridge is located in San Francisco Bay from the Pacific Ocean.

Q:When did the golden date bridge open?
