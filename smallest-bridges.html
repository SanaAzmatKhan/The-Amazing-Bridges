Henderson-wave Bridge
is a Wave-shaped 36-m pedestrian bridge connecting Mount Faber Park to Telok Blangah Hill Park.
Address: Henderson Road, Singapore 159557.

The Helix Bridge,
officially The Helix, and previously known as the Double Helix Bridge, is a pedestrian bridge linking Marina Centre with Marina South in the Marina Bay area in Singapore.
Address: Singapore.
Total length: 280 m.
Construction started: 2007.
Location: Downtown Core.
Bridge type: Footbridge.

The Infinity Bridge
is a public pedestrian and cycle footbridge across the River Tees in the borough of Stockton-on-Tees in the north-east of England.
Address: Thornaby, Stockton-on-Tees TS18 2NL, UK.
Construction started: June 2007.
Total length: 240 m.
Location: Stockton-on-Tees.
Bridge type: Arch bridge.

Somerset Bridge
is located at the western part of Bermuda in Sandys parish.
This is the world's smallest drawbridge.
Somerset bridge was originally constructed way back in 1620.
Although it was rebuilt in the 20th century.
Somerset Bridge is reputedly the smallest 'drawbridge'.
